ABOUT

Hello, My name is Kyler Renneker. I am 24 years old and have been a lover of technology for a majority of those 24 years. In my earliest encounters with the internet, I can remember being fascinated with how much information could be found in one place. I still vividly remember the dial-up sound when connecting to the internet in the mid-'90s. Technology, and the internet surrounding it has been an integral part of my life in nearly every aspect.

I built my first computer whilst in high school and I knew then that I wanted to work with technology for the rest of my life, expanding the field and being a part of a community of creators. So here I am, an eager, hungry, ambitious, tech-enthusiast delving into the world of development, always looking to expand my knowledge.
